01 Profile

Krishna Sagar is Managing Director at BlackMatter Builders & Developers, where he leads the strategic vision and operational execution for high-end residential and commercial projects. The group operates multiple brands — including TATWA, delivering Fuchsia Gardens in Irinjalakuda, and WAAW Mall, a commercial and lifestyle destination in the same town.

He is also Chief Executive Officer of Car Club Exotic Machines, sourcing, customizing, and delivering premium preowned automobiles for enthusiasts. A technologist by training, he previously served as CTO at Krysllio Technologies (2017–2024) and General Manager at Smart Infotech (2016–2017).
